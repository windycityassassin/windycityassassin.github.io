research
Physics-informed neural net for arterial wall stress.
Reproduces Holzapfel et al., Acta Biomaterialia 2022. Constitutive Artificial Neural Net (CANN) predicts strain-energy density from 14 microstructural features, recovers stress tensor by autodiff. R² = 0.9206 against paper-digitized data, inside the paper's reported 0.9-0.98 range. The live demo trains the same architecture on a synthetic HGO-flavored dataset (paper's data was not released) and serves it behind a Streamlit UI where you move the feature sliders and see the predicted stress-stretch curve update.

rubikscubetutorial
3D Rubik's cube simulator from scratch.
27 cubies rendered through the PyOpenGL fixed-function pipeline. Six face rotations via hand-written 2D rotation matrices and per-face sticker permutation tables. Mouse-drag camera orbit, 20-move random scramble. Built to actually understand 3D rotation math. The live demo is a single-file Three.js port with a Kociemba two-phase solver (computes a ~20-move solution from any scramble and animates it) and an inline tutorial covering notation and the Beginner's Method.
